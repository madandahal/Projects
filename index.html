Dr. Madan Dahal

Postdoctoral Researcher · Signal Processing & Machine Learning

University of Technology — Department of Electrical Engineering

I research the intersection of deep learning and next-generation wireless networks, focusing on interference management, beamforming, and resource allocation in 5G/6G mmWave systems. My work combines theoretical analysis with practical algorithm design using reinforcement learning and deep neural networks.
