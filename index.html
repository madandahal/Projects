Dr. Madan Dahal

Wireless Communciation, Signal Processing, Machine Learning, 5G, 6G

Villanova University — Department of Electrical Engineering

I research the intersection of machine learning and next-generation wireless networks, focusing on interference management, beamforming, and resource allocation in 5G/6G cellular systems. My work combines theoretical analysis with practical algorithm design using reinforcement learning and deep neural networks.

Enhancing interference mitigation via hybrid action space control deep reinforcement learning

Deep reinforcement learning for interference management in millimeter-wave networks

Applying the self-attention mechanism to jointly estimate time-varying mmWave channels across subcarriers, outperforming LSTM and pilot-based baselines at high Doppler.
